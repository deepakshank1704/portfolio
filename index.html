// 01 — About

Engineer at the silicon frontier

I'm a hardware engineer with a deep focus on signal and power integrity, mixed-signal IC design, and silicon validation. My work spans the full hardware stack — from RTL and ASIC tape-out through PCB bring-up and post-silicon characterization.

I've taped out an 8-bit R-2R DAC in TSMC 180nm, designed multi-layer PCBs in Altium, characterized RF circuits at 5 GHz using Keysight ADS and HFSS, and built Python-automated test benches for instrument control and data logging. I'm equally comfortable at a lab bench debugging power rail noise and at a terminal running a full RTL-to-GDS ASIC flow.

I'm currently seeking roles in signal integrity, hardware validation, circuit design, and PCB engineering — particularly in the San Jose / Bay Area.
